Herb Card Design

Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera is a succulent plant known for its medicinal properties. The gel from its leaves is often used to treat burns, skin conditions, and has various health benefits when consumed.

Basil

Basil is a fragrant herb commonly used in cooking, particularly in Italian cuisine. It has a sweet, peppery flavor and is a key ingredient in pesto sauce.

Rosemary

Rosemary is an evergreen herb with needle-like leaves. It is used as a culinary herb, particularly in Mediterranean cuisine, and is known for its aromatic and flavorful properties.

Lavender

Lavender is a flowering plant known for its aromatic purple flowers. It is used in aromatherapy, perfumes, and for its calming and relaxing properties.
